# Video Setup Guide

## How to Use YouTube Video as Background

### Option 1: Download and Host Locally (Recommended)

1. **Download the YouTube Video:**
   - Go to: https://ytmp3.cc/en13/
   - Paste the YouTube URL: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=t9-cMiEDNyk
   - Download as MP4 format
   - Choose high quality (1080p or 720p)

2. **Optimize the Video:**
   - Trim to 10-30 seconds (most impactful part)
   - Compress to under 10MB
   - Ensure it's in MP4 (H.264) format

3. **Place the Video:**
   - Rename downloaded file to: `car-showcase.mp4`
   - Place in: `/public/videos/car-showcase.mp4`

4. **The video will automatically play as background!**

### Option 2: Use YouTube Embed (Limited)

You can embed YouTube video, but it won't work as a true background due to:
- YouTube's autoplay restrictions
- No opacity control over embed
- Requires user interaction to play
- May show YouTube controls

### Option 3: Find Alternative Stock Videos

Free stock video sites with car footage:
- Pexels.com (free car videos)
- Pixabay.com (free automotive videos)
- Mixkit.co (free video clips)

## Recommended Video Specifications

- **Format**: MP4 (H.264)
- **Resolution**: 1920x1080
- **Duration**: 10-30 seconds
- **File Size**: Under 10MB
- **Content**: Car showcase, driving scenes

## Quick Start

1. Download the YouTube video using the link above
2. Trim to the best 15-20 seconds
3. Compress if needed
4. Save as `car-showcase.mp4` in `/public/videos/`
5. Refresh your website - video will play automatically!
